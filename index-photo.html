How can Ukraine ensure that the nation finally transforms into a fair, prosperous and democratic society in which rule of law is respected? The creation of a new social contract is required. But how to achieve it? On Dec. 2, the Tiger Conference will bring together government, business and civil society leaders from Ukraine and abroad to discuss this question.

Leading the discussion

Serving as an international platform for discussion of the most pressing challenges facing Ukraine, the conference will bring together over 300 government officials, business and civil society leaders from Ukraine and abroad. They will discuss:

  • Rule of Law
  • Energy
  • New Economy
  • Visionary Leadership
