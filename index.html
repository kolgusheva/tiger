History

The Tiger Conference is being held by the Kyiv Post for the fourth consecutive year.


  • 2012
  • 2013
  • 2014
Will the spleeping tiger awaken?

In 2012, the first Tiger Conference asked the question if Ukraine could become an East European tiger economy, following the example of the Asian states’ recent breakthroughs. Among the first speakers were Petro Poroshenko, Minister of Finance of Ukraine, Mikhel Saakashvili, President of Georgia, Mihai Moldovanu, Deputy Prime Minister of Republic of Moldova.

Zone of turbulence

The turbulent 2013 brought on new questions, shifting the main topic of the conference to Ukraine’s Future After the Vilnius Summit. Discussion explored Ukraine’s place in the world and prospects for development. The speakers included Jerome Vacher, IMF Representative to Ukraine, Jan Tombinski, Head of the EU Delegation to Ukraine, members of European Parliament.

One year after Euromaidan

The Tiger Conference 2014: Ukraine One Year After the Euromaidan Revolution took place when Ukraine’s survival has come under threat. The topics discussed were the nation’s most urgent challenges: security, information war, the need to capture new markets and the need to reform. Held in partnership with the World Economic Forum, the conference had among its speakers Lt.Gen. Hertling, former commander of the US Army in Europe, Natalia Jaresko, Founding Partner and CEO of Horizon Capital, Macon Phillips, Assistant to the President Barack Obama and Director of Digital Strategy.
